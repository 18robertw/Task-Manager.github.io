Sync & Backup

Transfer your data between devices by exporting and importing a backup file.

Export data
Download all your notes, tasks, categories, tags, contacts and templates as a JSON file. Use this to back up your data or transfer it to another device.
Import data
Import a backup file to restore or transfer your data. This will replace all current data on this device.
How to sync between devices
  1. On your desktop — click ⬇ Download backup or Copy to clipboard
  2. Transfer the file to your phone (AirDrop, email, iCloud, Google Drive, etc.) — or paste the clipboard text
  3. On your mobile — click ⬆ Choose file or Paste JSON
  4. Your data will be immediately available on this device
Your data syncs automatically every time you make a change. Last sync status is shown in the top bar.
